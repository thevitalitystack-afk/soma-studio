# LD-001 Thumbnail Design
## "Are YOU Ready to Face Your Shadows?"

### 🎨 Design Specifications
- **Dimensions:** 1600x1600 pixels
- **Format:** JPEG/PNG
- **Style:** Dark cinematic, moody aesthetic
- **Brand Colors:** Charcoal + Cream + Teal accents

### 📐 Layout Structure

**Main Visual:**
- Person standing in doorway of light
- Backlit by soft golden glow
- Silhouette with rim lighting
- Shadows on ground forming heart shape

**Text Overlay:**
- **Main Text:** "ARE YOU READY"
  - Font: Fraunces Bold
  - Size: 120px
  - Color: Cream (#F5F5DC)
  - Position: Top third

- **Sub Text:** "TO FACE YOUR SHADOWS?"
  - Font: League Spartan Bold
  - Size: 80px
  - Color: Teal (#00FFD1)
  - Position: Bottom third

**Accent Elements:**
- Small crescent moon icon
- Star particles (subtle)
- Gradient overlay (dark at edges)

### 🎨 Color Palette

| Element | Color | Hex |
|---------|-------|-----|
| Background | Deep Charcoal | #1A1A1A |
| Text Primary | Cream | #F5F5DC |
| Text Accent | Teal | #00FFD1 |
| Overlay | Black (30% opacity) | rgba(0,0,0,0.3) |
| Highlights | Soft Gold | #D4AF37 |

### 📱 Text Hierarchy

**Primary:** "ARE YOU READY"
- Large, commanding presence
- Centered at top
- Teal outline for visibility

**Secondary:** "TO FACE YOUR SHADOWS?"
- Smaller but still prominent
- Bottom center
- Clean, sans-serif

**Style Notes:**
- High contrast for mobile visibility
- Minimal text (YouTube requirement)
- Strong focal point
- Emotional appeal
