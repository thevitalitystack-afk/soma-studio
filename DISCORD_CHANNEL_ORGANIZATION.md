## Channel Naming Convention

**Format:** `#category-name`
**Style:** lowercase with hyphens
**Purpose:** Clear, searchable, organized

## Bot Permissions by Category

### Working Channels
- **Post:** Research findings, updates
- **Read:** All messages
- **Manage:** Pin important updates

### Agent Communication
- **Post:** Status updates, task assignments
- **Read:** All messages
- **Mention:** @Luna, @Atlas, etc.

### Research & Analytics
- **Post:** Daily/weekly reports
- **Read:** Team questions
- **React:** With relevant emojis

### Creative Assets
- **Post:** Concepts, drafts
- **Read:** Feedback
- **Edit:** Pin guidelines

### Technical
- **Post:** Error alerts, status
- **Read:** Team questions
- **Manage:** Bot commands

### General
- **Post:** Announcements
- **Read:** All messages
- **React:** Standard reactions

## Automation Rules

### Posting Schedule
- **Research:** Every 6 hours (6 AM, 12 PM, 6 PM, 12 AM)
- **Status Updates:** Real-time
- **Daily Reports:** 9 AM
- **Weekly Summary:** Sundays 9 AM

### Notification Settings
- **@mentions:** High priority
- **Keywords:** "urgent", "bug", "deploy"
- **Bots:** All bot messages visible
