## 🎬 **Content Production Workflows**

### Shorts Workflow (30-60s)

**1. Research Phase**
- BEACON identifies trending spirituality topics
- Keywords: mindfulness, manifestation, anxiety relief
- Title variants for A/B testing

**2. Script Creation**
- Hook: First 3 seconds, punchy beats
- Teal captions for visibility
- Structure: Hook → 9-second beats → Close + CTA

**3. Production**
- Voice: ElevenLabs (George voice)
- Captions: Teal with clean font
- B-roll: Moody photography, slow motion

**4. Optimization**
- High retention hooks
- Top-of-funnel content
- CTA for long-form videos

### Long-form Workflow (8-25 min)

**1. Research Phase**
- BEACON: Keywords, trending angles, title variants
- Competitor analysis
- Content gap identification

**2. Direction Approval**
- HELM: Approves concept and structure
- Locks title, thumbnail, hook
- Sets chapter markers

**3. Script Writing**
- VERSE: Full script creation
- Hook → Promise → Chapters → Synthesis → CTA
- SOMA voice: Warm, precise, slightly austere

**4. Voice Production**
- CHORUS: ElevenLabs VO prompts
- George voice for premium feel
- Pacing and emotional markers

**5. Video Editing**
- BLADE: Edit decision list
- Pacing: Slow, contemplative
- B-roll: Moody photography cues
- Music: Ambient, minimal

**6. Publishing**
- SEO optimization
- Thumbnail: Dark cinematic
- Description with timestamps
- Tags and playlist placement

## 🔄 **Agent Roles & Responsibilities**

### BEACON (Research)
- Monitors spirituality/psychology trends
- Identifies high-performing topics
- Generates title variants
- Analyzes competitor content

### HELM (Direction)
- Approves content concepts
- Locks creative direction
- Ensures brand alignment
- Makes final decisions

### VERSE (Writing)
- Writes engaging scripts
- Crafts compelling hooks
- Structures chapters
- Creates CTAs

### CHORUS (Voice)
- Generates VO prompts
- Optimizes voice parameters
- Ensures consistent tone
- Manages voice assets

### BLADE (Editing)
- Creates edit lists
- Specifies b-roll cues
- Manages pacing
- Coordinates music drops
